A Message From Daniel

When I started this company, I aspired to create the best coffee imaginable. This is why every morning I painstakingly board my private jet and fly to the Amazon Jungle, and pick every coffee bean by hand. After 48 hours straight of working, my hands don't feel so good, but I do it for the people. The only thing that I consume during these long hours working in the jungle is coffee from "Daniel's Coffee". This is how you know that coffee from "Daniel's Coffee" is the real deal.
